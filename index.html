About Junco

A Lakewood company, built on showing up.

Junco was started because the junk removal market in Cleveland had a reliability problem. Companies that don't answer the phone. Crews that show up late, or not at all. Jobs left half done. We built Junco to be the opposite of all of that.

We're named after the junco bird — small, scrappy, and built for Ohio winters. It shows up when others don't, works efficiently, and doesn't make a fuss. That's exactly what we do for our customers every single day.

We're based right here in Lakewood. When you call us, you're talking to a neighbor — not a call center. We know these streets, we care about this community, and we'll treat your home with the same respect we'd want for our own.
